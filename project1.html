USA Fun Map

An interactive map designed to make learning about US states engaging and informative.

Role

As a front-end developer, I collaborated closely with a team to research and implement interactive features, ensuring a user-friendly experience while navigating through the map.

Challenges

  • Adapted project scope due to time constraints from a global interactive map to a US-centric one.
  • Modified approach to comply with web scraping restrictions and platform policies.
  • Implemented Django for data management to fit within budget constraints, avoiding costly databases.
  • Redirected focus to integrate Wikipedia-sourced data for state statistics.
